⚙️ Pipeline Features
  • Headless Playwright automation bypasses JavaScript-rendered Shopee pages
  • Configurable keyword targeting — swap product category in one variable
  • Automated scroll + pagination to capture full listing pages
  • Data cleaning: strip currency symbols, parse sold counts, normalise ratings
  • Deduplication and null-handling with Pandas pipelines
  • One-click export to structured Excel workbook
🔍 Market Insights (Camping Shoes)
Price Spread: Wide range across sellers — budget listings cluster below ₱500 while premium options exceed ₱2,000.
Ratings: Most top-sold items maintain ≥ 4.5 stars, indicating quality expectation in outdoor gear.
Volume Leaders: A small subset of shops account for a disproportionate share of total units sold.
Keyword Patterns: Listings with "waterproof" and "trail" in titles show consistently higher engagement.
